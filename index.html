Who we are

We are a movement of LGBTQ people at Sac State seeking to explore who God is in our lives. While many people think that being LGBTQ and being Christian are exclusive, we disagree. We have people of many faith backgrounds with different ways of expericing the intersection of faith and sexuality.

What do we believe?

That there is room for everyone in the kingdom of God.

That Christ has infinite love and grace for all people. In light of this belief we view homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, racism, and ableism as parts of this fallen world.

That Christ calls each of us to honor him in different ways. We respect the calling of each individual whether they live in celibacy or persue loving relationships.

That Jesus lived as a perfect example of who we should be. He died and then rose from the dead and will come again.
